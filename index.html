Why and who

Hello, I'm Jon. I've been improvising for five years and doing theater for seven. Over the years, I've learned, taught, performed and explored all the different aspects of improv that fascinate me.

In this course, I will try to fit everything I know into eight weeks. From the very basics to the most complex scene work. My hope is to give you the framework from which you can grow as an improviser.

I also want to make sure that you get to play as much as possible because practice makes perfection. So expect a 30% theory, 70% practice kind of course.
